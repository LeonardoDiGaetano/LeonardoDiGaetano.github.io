Teaching

My teaching philosophy is centered around fostering a collaborative and interactive learning environment where students can actively engage with the material and develop critical thinking skills. I strongly believe that the key to effective learning is to make the subject matter relevant and engaging for the students, and to provide them with opportunities to apply what they have learned in real-world contexts.
